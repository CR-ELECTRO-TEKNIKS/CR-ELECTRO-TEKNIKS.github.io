Test Terminal Blocks 3 phase 4 Wire (Compact)

Product Description

The Test Terminal Blocks are moulded in house out of Grade I Electrical grade Bakelite powder with latest Automatic machines . The Cover is moulded with either Bakelite or Transparent Policarbonate (depending on customer requirement).Flash over is eliminated by providing barriers (Ribs) between adjacent terminals. The cover is placed in position by Brass knurling nuts with locking and sealing arrangements. All terminals and metal components are made of brass with Nickle plating.

Applications

Site testing can be made without disconnecting the existing wiring leading to the consumers or to the meters by the Test Terminal Blocks connected with Ammeters, K.WH Meters, Maximum Demand Indicators, Trivector Meters,etc. Any connections of current or voltage coils of testing instruments or shorting of the CT secondary winding can be easily and quickly made. They are therefore used in connections with stationary high voltage measuring units.They can also be used for testing relays and other testing equipments by making slight modifications.

Terminal Arrangement

There are three voltage terminals and a neutral terminal along with three sets of current terminals.The voltage terminals are designed for incoming and out going leads to be connected or disconnected by means of a movable link. Provision in the Bakelite body allows two or more wires to be connected if so required. Screws are used to connect the links to the terminals. When many meters are tested, their current coils should be connected in series.If meters are to be tested individually, separate Test Terminal Blocks should be used for each meter

Technical Details

  • Voltage Rating : 1100V
  • Current Rating : 50Amps continuous at 50 degree Centigrade
  • Consectetur
  • Diameter of entries : 4 mm
