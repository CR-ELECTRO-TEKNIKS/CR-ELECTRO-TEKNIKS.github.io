FUSE Puller

Product Description

We specialize in premium quality Fuse Pullers that is made from high grade Phenolic Molding for better Strength and supreme electrical insulation. Our Fuse Puller is equipped with advanced technology and thus offers high and precise performance. The easy to install, use and maintain puller offered by us has cemented our place among the celebrated Fuse Puller Manufacturer and Supplier from Bangalore,Karnataka.

NH FUSE PULLER is used to safely insert and remove the NH Fuse Link of size 000,00,1,2,3 from its Base. CRET Fuse Puller is electrically insulated and safe to handle with hands.
CRET Fuse Puller is designed in accordance with the standards of IS 13703:1993 and IEC : 6029.

Technical Details

  • Operating Voltage: upto 1000V
  • Product type: Ni
  • Suitable NH Fuse Link Size: 000,00,1,2,3
  • Current Rating: 50-60 Amps
  • Fuse Type: Cartridge Fuse, Blade Fuse
